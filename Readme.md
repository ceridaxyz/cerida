leverag doe Must add (the ticket is misleading without them):

Knockout price — the defining number of a turbo product. Show it in both units: "Knocked out if YES falls to 40.5¢ (≈ BTC $61,300)". Update live as the slider moves — watching the knockout creep toward entry as λ rises is exactly the risk education the §6.1 policy wants. Max win — full reserve means the max payout is known and locked: "Max win $54 · Max loss $10." That's the sportsbook slip element, it's honest, and it's a differentiator (perps can't print this). Expiry + auto-flatten notice — these are expiring markets: a countdown ("settles in 41m") and "position auto-closes 2 min before expiry." A perp trader will be genuinely surprised otherwise. Dynamic slider cap — 50× must not be reachable on an ATM 0DTE market. Cap the slider at λ_max(p₀, τ) (ITM 10–25×, ATM 2–6×, OTM 1–3×) with the rest of the track grayed/warned ("knockout ~97% likely"). Soft cap is fine — it's trader protection, not solvency.
